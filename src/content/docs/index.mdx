## Getting Started

New to MyGPTAssistants? Here's how to get started:

1. **Create an Assistant** - Head to the [Playground](/assistants/playground/) to build your first AI assistant
2. **Train with Data** - Upload documents in [Data Sources](/assistants/data-sources/) to give your bot knowledge
3. **Customize Appearance** - Brand your widget in [Appearance](/assistants/appearance/) settings
4. **Deploy** - [Embed](/assistants/embed/) your bot on your website or connect via [Integrations](/assistants/integrations/)
5. **Monitor** - Track conversations and performance in [Analytics](/assistants/analytics/)

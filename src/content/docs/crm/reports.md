# Reports

Get insights into your business with comprehensive reporting.

## Report Types

### Contact Reports

- Total contacts over time
- Contact sources
- Geographic distribution
- Tag distribution
- Growth rate

### Conversation Reports

- Volume by bot
- Volume by channel
- Resolution rates
- Average duration
- Peak hours

### Ticket Reports

- Open vs resolved
- By priority
- By assignee
- Resolution time
- SLA compliance

### Campaign Reports

- Open rates
- Click rates
- Conversion tracking
- Best performing content
- Optimal send times

### Lead Reports

- Lead sources
- Pipeline stages
- Conversion rates
- Lead velocity
- Win/loss analysis

## Building Reports

### Quick Reports

Pre-built reports ready to use:
1. Go to **Reports**
2. Select report type
3. Choose date range
4. View results

### Custom Reports

Build your own:
1. Click **+ New Report**
2. Select data source
3. Choose metrics
4. Add dimensions
5. Apply filters
6. Save report

### Report Components

**Metrics** (what to measure):
- Count, sum, average
- Percentages
- Rates

**Dimensions** (how to group):
- By date/time
- By category
- By user/assignee
- By segment

**Filters** (what to include):
- Date range
- Specific values
- Conditions

### Dashboards

Combine multiple reports:

## Scheduling

### Automated Reports

Schedule regular delivery:
1. Open report
2. Click **Schedule**
3. Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
4. Add recipients
5. Save

### Export Formats

- PDF - For sharing
- CSV - For analysis
- Excel - For manipulation

## Best Practices

1. **Focus on actionable metrics** - Track what you can influence
2. **Set benchmarks** - Know what "good" looks like
3. **Review regularly** - Weekly or monthly cadence
4. **Share insights** - Keep team informed
5. **Iterate** - Refine reports over time
